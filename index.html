South African Boerboel

The South African Boerboel is a large, powerful, and intelligent breed known for its exceptional guarding abilities. Originally bred to protect farms in South Africa, these dogs are highly protective of their families and properties. Despite their confident and territorial nature, they are also gentle and affectionate with their loved ones.

French Bulldog

The French Bulldog is a small, charming, and affectionate breed, recognizable by its distinctive "bat-like" ears. Originally bred in France as companion dogs, they have become popular pets worldwide due to their friendly and sociable personalities. French Bulldogs are low-energy and well-suited for apartment living, making them excellent companions for both families and individuals.

Whelping Services

Whelping services provide specialized care for pregnant dogs before, during, and after the birth of their puppies. They ensure the health and safety of both the mother and her litter by offering round-the-clock care, monitoring health, assisting with the birthing process, and providing post-labor care. Whelping services also handle feeding, socialization, and veterinary checkups for the puppies until they are ready for their new homes.

Stud Services

Stud services involve providing a male dog (stud) for breeding purposes. These services ensure the stud dog is healthy, has a good temperament, and meets specific breed standards. Owners seeking stud services pair their female dog (dam) with an appropriate stud to produce puppies with desirable traits.

About us

Our Company

With two decades of dedication to the Boerboel breed, we've established ourselves as a trusted name in the world of dog breeding. All of our puppies are lovingly raised on our farm and in our home, ensuring they are well-socialized and nurtured from the very beginning. Melinda, a trailblazer in the Boerboel community, was among the first three breeders to introduce these remarkable dogs to the United States. Our commitment to quality is reflected in our meticulous selection process, prioritizing exceptional bloodlines and robust health. Our dogs undergo rigorous health screenings, including hip and elbow testing, to ensure they meet the highest standards. We also evaluate their athletic abilities and social behavior with other animals, guaranteeing well-rounded and healthy companions. With us, you can trust that you're getting a Boerboel raised with expertise, love, and a deep understanding of the breed.
